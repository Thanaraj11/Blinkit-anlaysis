# Blinkit Data Analytics: Unlocking Sales Performance & Customer Trends

---

## Project Overview

**Objective:** Analyze Blinkit's retail data to uncover business insights
**Dataset:** 8,523 records, 12 variables
**Tools:** Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)

**Key Areas Analyzed:**
- Product performance by fat content
- Sales distribution by item type
- Business KPIs and customer ratings

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation

**Challenge:** Inconsistent "Item Fat Content" categories
**Solution:** Standardized to two categories:
- Before: `['Regular', 'Low Fat', 'low fat', 'LF', 'reg']`
- After: `['Regular', 'Low Fat']`

**Why it matters:** Clean data = Reliable insights

## Analysis 1: Sales by Fat Content

**Key Finding:** Low Fat products dominate revenue

- **Low Fat:** ~65% of total sales
- **Regular:** ~35% of total sales

**Business Insight:** Health-conscious trend is driving sales
**Recommendation:** Focus marketing and inventory on Low Fat products
